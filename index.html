 One day I wanted to make my own processor... and I did!

minCPU is a family of small, mostly-empty CPU designs. Despite their simplicity, you might still manage to run impressive things on them.

Example

A simple fibonacci function: (note: minASM-16 is not available yet) 


fib:
  CGT WR0, $0
  JC success
  ST WR1, WR0
  RET

success:
  DEC WR0
  CALL fib
  ST WR2, WR1
  DEC WR0
  CALL fib
  ADD WR1, WR2
  RET

_main:
  ST WR0, 10
  CALL fib
  HLT
Easy, I know.
